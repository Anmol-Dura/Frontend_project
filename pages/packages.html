Adventurous Alberta

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Calgary

Upon arrival, explore downtown Calgary's vibrant streets lined with shops, restaurants, and cultural landmarks. Spend the night in one of the city's cozy accommodations.

Day 2: Calgary to Canmore

Embark on a scenic drive from Calgary to Canmore, soaking in the majestic views of the Canadian Rockies along the way. Spend the day exploring Canmore's charming downtown area and picture surroundings before settling in for the night

Day 3: Canmore to Banff

Travel to Banff, a world-renowned destination, browse local boutiques, and visit fascinating museums. Retire to your accommodations for a restful night in Banff.

Day 4: Banff-Lake Minnewanka

Journey to Lake Minnewanka, where crystal-clear waters and rugged mountain scenery await. Enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, boating, or picnicking along the lakeshore. Return to Banff in the evening for a relaxing night in the heart of the Rockies.

Day 5: Explore Banff National

Embark on an adventure through Banff National Park, marveling at natural wonders like Johnston Canyon's waterfalls and the turquoise hues of Moraine Lake. Capture stunning photographs and immerse yourself in the park's pristine wilderness. We will venture to nearby Lake Louise for accommodation.

Day 6: Lake Louise

Discover the iconic beauty of Lake Louise, surrounded by towering peaks and serene alpine landscapes. Engage in activities such as hiking, canoeing, or simply admiring the breathtaking scenery. Wind down with a peaceful evening in Lake Louise or nearby accommodations.

Marvellous Ontario

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Niagara Falls

Arrive in Niagara Falls and spend the day exploring the iconic waterfalls from various viewpoints in Niagara Falls State Park. Witness the breathtaking beauty of the falls, enjoy a Maid of the Mist boat tour, and dine with a view of the illuminated cascades.

Day 2: Niagara Fall Attractions

Experience the power of Niagara Falls up close with a Maid of the Mist boat tour and visit attractions like Journey Behind the Falls and the Butterfly Conservatory. Explore Clifton Hill's entertainment district in the evening, offering a variety of dining and entertainment options.

Day 3: Travel to Toronto

Depart Niagara Falls and journey to Toronto, Canada's vibrant metropolis. Visit the iconic CN Tower for panoramic views of the city skyline and Lake Ontario. Explore the Harbourfront area, home to cultural attractions, restaurants, and shops, offering a picturesque setting for an evening stroll.

Day 4: Exploring Toronto

Discover Toronto's cultural riches with a visit to the Royal Ontario Museum (ROM) and explore the diverse exhibits showcasing art, history, and natural wonders. Wander through the historic Distillery District, lined with charming shops and cafes, before experiencing the city's lively nightlife in trendy neighborhoods.

Day 5: Blue Montain Resort

Embark on a scenic journey to Blue Mountain Resort near Collingwood, offering an array of outdoor activities amidst stunning natural landscapes. Spend the day hiking, zip-lining, or mountain biking, and unwind in the evening with a relaxing spa session or fine dining experience at the resort.

Eastern Escapade

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Montreal

Begin your journey in Montreal, a city known for its vibrant culture and historic architecture. Explore Old Montreal, stroll along cobblestone streets, and visit iconic landmarks such as Notre-Dame Basilica and the Old Port. Indulge in French-inspired cuisine at a local bistro.

Day 2: Discovering Montreal

Embark on a guided tour of Montreal's highlights, including Mount Royal Park for panoramic city views, the Montreal Museum of Fine Arts, and bustling neighborhoods like Plateau-Mont-Royal and Mile End. Experience the city's culinary scene with a food tour or visit Jean-Talon Market.

Day 3: Travel to Quebec City

Journey to Quebec City, a UNESCO World Heritage site renowned for its European charm and fortified walls. Explore the historic district of Old Quebec, visit Château Frontenac, and wander through Petit-Champlain, one of North America's oldest streets. Enjoy traditional Quebecois cuisine for dinner.

Day 4: Exploring Quebec

Delve deeper into Quebec City's history with visits to the Citadel, Place Royale, and the Plains of Abraham. Experience the city's cultural scene with a visit to the Musée de la civilisation or attend a performance at the Grand Théâtre de Québec.

Day 5: Hallifax

Travel to Halifax, Nova Scotia's capital, known for its maritime heritage and scenic waterfront. Explore the historic Halifax Citadel National Historic Site, stroll along the waterfront boardwalk, and visit the Canadian Museum of Immigration at Pier 21. Enjoy fresh seafood for dinner at a local restaurant.

Day 6: Hallifax Attractions

Discover Halifax's attractions, including the Maritime Museum of the Atlantic, showcasing the city's seafaring history, and the Halifax Public Gardens, a tranquil oasis in the heart of downtown. Explore the vibrant neighborhoods of North End and Agricola Street for artisanal shops and cafes.
