Calgary - Canmore - Banff

Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival in Calgary

Upon arrival, explore downtown Calgary's vibrant streets lined with shops, restaurants, and cultural landmarks. Spend the night in one of the city's cozy accommodations.

Day 2: Calgary to Canmore

Embark on a scenic drive from Calgary to Canmore, soaking in the majestic views of the Canadian Rockies along the way. Spend the day exploring Canmore's charming downtown area and picture surroundings before settling in for the night

Day 3: Canmore to Banff

Travel to Banff, a world-renowned destination, browse local boutiques, and visit fascinating museums. Retire to your accommodations for a restful night in Banff.

Day 4: Banff National Park Day 1 - Lake Minnewanka

Journey to Lake Minnewanka, where crystal-clear waters and rugged mountain scenery await. Enjoy outdoor activities such as hiking, boating, or picnicking along the lakeshore. Return to Banff in the evening for a relaxing night in the heart of the Rockies.

Day 5: Explore Banff National Park Day 2

Embark on an adventure through Banff National Park, marveling at natural wonders like Johnston Canyon's waterfalls and the turquoise hues of Moraine Lake. Capture stunning photographs and immerse yourself in the park's pristine wilderness. We will venture to nearby Lake Louise for accommodation.

Day 6: Lake Louise

Discover the iconic beauty of Lake Louise, surrounded by towering peaks and serene alpine landscapes. Engage in activities such as hiking, canoeing, or simply admiring the breathtaking scenery. Wind down with a peaceful evening in Lake Louise or nearby accommodations.
